Philippine Art History

The Spoliarium - Juan Luna

Painted in 1884, this masterpiece serves as a powerful symbol of Filipino social, moral, and political life during the Spanish colonization.

Fernando Amorsolo

Known as the "Grand Old Man of Philippine Art," he was the first National Artist, celebrating rural life and the Filipino aesthetic.
