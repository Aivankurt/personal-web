FILIPINO ART MASTERS

FERNANDO AMORSOLO

Fernando Amorsolo was the first-ever National Artist of the Philippines... Today, his legacy lives on in the National Museum and private collections.

JUAN LUNA

Antonio Luna was a giant of 19th-century art... his masterpiece 'Spoliarium' won gold in Madrid, proving Filipino excellence to the world.

GELONIMATION

Gelonimation is a pioneer of the "Storytime Animation" genre in the local scene, blending humor with relatable Filipino experiences.

ABE ANIMATED

Abe Animated is a prominent figure in the local independent animation scene, known for unique character designs and storytelling.
